AUG 27 2025

Presentation Final Year Project

Today, I had the honor of presenting my Final Year Project entitled “Hotel Revenue Optimization System using Genetic Algorithm” in front of my supervisor, Assoc. Prof. Dr. Nor Atiqah Sia, as well as Dr. Abdullah Dr. Ezzatul Akmal Kamaru Zaman and Assoc. Prof. Dr. Sofianita Mutalib. My project focuses on leveraging Genetic Algorithms, an evolutionary computing technique to optimize hotel room pricing across different seasons with the goal of maximizing revenue. To bring this concept to life, I developed a web-based system using Java (NetBeans) called EzBoost, where the optimization results can be visualized and tested in real time.

I am truly grateful to my supervisor, Assoc. Prof. Dr. Nor Atiqah Sia, for her continuous guidance and for trusting me with access to a real dataset from Royale Chulan Kuala Lumpur. This experience has been both rewarding and inspiring, bridging the gap between academic research and real-world industry application.
